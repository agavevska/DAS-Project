This application will enable users to review, search, and authenticate data related to the stock exchange in the Republic of Macedonia. When selecting a specific ticker, the price of the last transaction, minimum, maximum, and average price, percentage change, as well as many other details related to the chosen issuer will be displayed. Users will be able to see the stock movement of each company through graphical representations. This visualization aids in decision-making and facilitates analysis.
